**SOCGS** (Second-Order Conditional Gradient Sliding) combines second-order methods with projection-free conditional gradient algorithms. It achieves quadratic convergence on polytope-constrained problems while avoiding expensive projections.

Published in the *Fields Institute Communications Series on Data Science and Optimization* (2020).

[Paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/2002.08907) · [GitHub](https://github.com/ZIB-IOL/SOCGS) · [Blog](https://www.pokutta.com/blog/research/2020/02/20/SOCGS.html)
